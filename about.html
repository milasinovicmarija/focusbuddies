About us

FocusBuddies is a productivity platform designed for students, professionals, and creatives who struggle to stay consistent when working alone.

By combining time tracking with shared focus sessions, the app creates a sense of accountability without pressure. You decide your goals, choose your focus partners, and work in a calm, distraction-reduced environment.
