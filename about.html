About us

First off, for legal reasons, this website is a mockup for my law course.

Marks:

  1. Word mark: FocusBuddies

    The name "FocusBuddies" consists of two words that perfectly show what our primary service is: "Focus", which reflects the productivity and time-tracking aspect of our service, and "Buddies", which reflects the communal part, the shared tracking and sense of responsibility the sharing brings. Together they encapsulate our brand perfectly.

    Nice classification: Class 9 (Computer and Software Products and Electrical and Scientific Products), Class 42 (Computer and Software Services and Scientific Services)

  2. Word mark: PlanShare

    "PlanShare" is used to identify a feature within the FocusBuddies platform, currently available to pro customers only. The name combines the terms "Plan", to repeat that it is focused on productivity and plans, and "Share", to signify the integration of shared plans and calendars. The PlanShare feature allows users to create shared schedules, synchronize calendars with focusbuddies, and vote on plans or time slots.

    Nice classification: Class 9 (Computer and Software Products), Class 42 (Computer and Software Services)

  3. Symbol mark

    The circular logo featuring the letter "f" for focus and a stick figure representing a "buddy", which was freely downloaded from vecteezy.com and is as such used under the listed license, that allows for use as long as they are credited by linking where possible. They are also credited in the alt caption of the logo. Using both of the "initials" of our service, "fb", was deemed risky since they are widely interpreted as a very popular social media abbreviation. Hence a more visual approach was taken. The placement of the logo itself is very flexible.

    Nice classification: Class 9 (Computer and Software Products and Electrical and Scientific Products), Class 35 (Advertising, Business and Retail Servicesa), Class 42 (Computer and Software Services and Scientific Services)

  4. Color mark

    The colors #6d28d9 and #ede9fe are used consistently throughout the user interface to highlight interactive elements and create a calm visual hierarchy. For accessibility reasons, color is never the sole means of conveying information. We are in no way claiming our right over the use of these colors and we are not using any colors that have been trademarked.

    Nice classification: Class 9 (Computer and Software Products and Electrical and Scientific Products), Class 42 (Computer and Software Services and Scientific Services)
