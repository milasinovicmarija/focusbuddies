Accessibility Statement

FocusBuddies is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 at level AA of conformity.

To support accessibility, FocusBuddies has implemented the following measures:

  • Clear and consistent navigation across all pages
  • Sufficient color contrast
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and interactive elements
  • Alternative text for images
  • Adaptability to all screen sizes

While we aim for full accessibility, embedded third-party content may not fully meet all accessibility requirements.
