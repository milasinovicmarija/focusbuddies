About Us

What is FocusBuddies?

FocusBuddies is a productivity tracker designed for students, professionals, and creatives who struggle to stay consistent when working alone.

The app creates a sense of accountability, but without pressure. Seeing your buddies stay productive helps you stay on the right track too!

Make the Switch to Dynamic Focus Tracking

Physical planners and notebooks have long been tools for organizing daily tasks. However, paper-based systems often fail to adapt to changing schedules and collaborative work.

Dynamic productivity trackers like FocusBuddies offer real-time developments and changes that better support the evolving problems that are thrown at us daily.
