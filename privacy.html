4. Third-Party Services

We may use tools like analytics services, but no personal customer data will be given to Third-Party services.

5. Data Security

We protect your data as best as possible. You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this website.
